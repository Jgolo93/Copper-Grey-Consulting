Our Story

Built on experience, driven by results, focused on your success

Copper Grey Consulting was founded with a simple mission: to make professional project management accessible to small businesses. After witnessing countless small businesses struggle with project failures, budget overruns, and missed deadlines, we knew there had to be a better way.

With over 20 years of experience in enterprise project management, we've adapted proven methodologies specifically for small business needs. We understand that small businesses don't have the luxury of large budgets or dedicated project teams, which is why we've created scalable, cost-effective solutions that deliver real results.
